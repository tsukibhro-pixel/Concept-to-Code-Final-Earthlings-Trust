Because Every Paw Deserves Love


Earthlings Trust is an animal welfare NGO based in Delhi-NCR, dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming sick, handicapped, and abandoned dogs. Founded in December 2018 by Sagarika Banerjee and Ditej Garg, we have cared for over 5000 dogs and currently provide permanent shelter to 350+ dogs.
