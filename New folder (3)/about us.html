ABOUT US

At Earthlings Trust, we believe that every life matters, especially those of our furry friends. Founded in December 2018 by Sagarika Banerjee and Ditej Garg, our mission is to provide a safe haven for stray dogs and other animals in need, ensuring they receive the care and compassion they deserve. Based in Noida, India, we have grown from humble beginnings to become a leading dog NGO in Noida, dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating animals.

Compassion, Care, and a Second Chance for Every Stray.

We envision a world where every dog is treated with kindness and respect, free from neglect and abandonment. Our shelter is dedicated to:

  • Saving Dogs from Danger: Rescuing dogs from perilous situations, including those affected by floods or abandonment.
  • Providing Necessary Treatments: Ensuring every dog receives vaccinations, sterilizations, and medical care tailored to their unique needs.
  • Finding Forever Homes: Facilitating dog adoption in Noida and surrounding areas, helping our furry friends find loving families.
  • Resolving Human-Animal Conflicts: Promoting peaceful coexistence between humans and animals through education and outreach.
